about us

A pioneering technical and vocational education to enable graduates to be more competitive in the local and global job market.

The overall goal of the educational programs and other activities covered by the Khan Yunis Training College is to enable Palestinian refugees from the age groups of 14 to 22 years in the Gaza Strip in general and in the southern region in particular, to overcome the consequences of the disengagement and the Israeli withdrawal Social and economic fields, by providing targeted age groups with education and vocational and technical training in order to obtain packages of competitive educational and training skills that help them more efficiently to contribute to the development of the local community in the Gaza Strip and the region.
